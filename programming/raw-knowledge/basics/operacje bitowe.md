#tech-area/basics 

-   `shl(bits)` – signed shift left

-   `ushr(bits)` – unsigned shift right
    
-   `and(bits)` – bitwise **and**
    
-   `or(bits)` – bitwise **or**
    
-   `xor(bits)` – bitwise **xor**
    
-   `inv()` – bitwise inversion
